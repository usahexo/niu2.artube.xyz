Do you know the difference between a door handle and a door knob? Most people don’t realize that there is a difference, but there is!

A door knob is mainly used for turning the knob to open or close the door. A door handle usually has a lever that you push down to open the door.

door handles are usually made out of brass or porcelain, while door handles are typically made out of metal or plastic.

Knobs are often found on older homes, while handles are more popular on newer homes. Some people prefer knobs because they are easier to grip, while others prefer handles because they are more ergonomic.

Why is it important to use the correct type of door handle or knob for your door?

There are a few reasons why it’s important to use the correct type of door handle or knob for your door. One reason is that improper installation of a door handle or knob can cause the latch mechanism to bind and prevent the door from opening or closing. Additionally, using the incorrect type of door handle or knob can damage the door or the surrounding wall.

One of the most important things to consider when selecting a door handle or knob is the weight of the door. A standard interior door typically weighs about 80 pounds, so it’s important to use a handle or knob that is designed to support that weight. A lever-type handle is ideal for a standard interior door, as it’s specifically designed to support more weight than a traditional doorknob.

It’s also important to use the correct type of handle or knob for your door’s configuration. For example, if you have a double-door configuration, you’ll need to use a pair of handles or knobs that are matched in size and style.

If you’re not sure which type of handle or knob is right for your door, consult with a professional contractor who can help you select the right hardware for your application.
